## Production Notes

### Typography
- Primary: SF Pro Display or system sans-serif equivalent
- Weight: Light to Regular for body, Medium for emphasis
- Size: Large — text should be readable at mobile viewport. Minimum 48pt equivalent for key lines.
- Color: White (#ffffff) for primary text, silver (#c0c0c0) for secondary, blue-silver (#8fa3b0) for accent/emphasis

### Motion
- Fade-in speed: 0.5–0.8 seconds. Never instant. Never bouncy.
- Hold time: Each text card holds for 2–4 seconds minimum. Let the words breathe.
- Transitions: Fade only. No wipes, no slides, no zooms.
- Screen recordings: 1:1 from app. No zooming into UI elements. Show the full interface at natural scale.

### Audio
- No voiceover. Text carries the message.
- Background: Minimal ambient tone only. Reference: Brian Eno's "Music for Airports" energy level — present but not demanding.
- The silence IS the brand. Gaps between audio elements are intentional.

### Colour Palette
- Background: #0a0a0a (primary), #1a1a1a (secondary/lighter panels)
- Text: #ffffff (primary), #c0c0c0 (secondary), #8fa3b0 (accent)
- No bright colours at any point. No gradients beyond very subtle dark-to-slightly-less-dark.

### Platform Exports
- 15s: 1080x1920 (Stories/Reels), 1920x1080 (YouTube pre-roll), 1080x1080 (Feed square)
- 30s: Same dimensions
- 60s: 1920x1080 (YouTube), 1080x1920 (long-form vertical)
