Section 2
Landing Page Scroll Narrative
6 sections in scroll order, telling the full Capsule story from privacy through to action.
01 — PRIVACY

Your audio never leaves.

Capsule processes transcription on your device. There is no server between your voice and your transcript. No upload. No intermediary. No exception — unless you explicitly choose cloud credits for longer files.

02 — HOW IT WORKS

On-device AI. Off-device? Only if you say so.

Local mode uses your machine's processor — free, forever, no account required. For longer recordings or higher accuracy, cloud credits use the same APIs as every other tool. The difference is what we charge you.

03 — COST TRANSPARENCY

We'll show you the receipt.

AI transcription costs roughly €0.006 per minute at wholesale. Most tools charge €0.05 to €0.10 — a 700% to 1,500% markup. Capsule charges €0.0066. Here is the math. Here is the margin. That is the entire business model.

04 — THE EXPERIENCE

No bot joins your call.

Capsule captures audio directly from your device. It does not inject a participant into your meeting. Nobody sees a notification. Nobody wonders who is recording. The capture is invisible because the capture is local.

05 — BUILT FOR

Built for the people with the most to protect.

Journalists protecting sources. Researchers handling sensitive interviews. Therapists, lawyers, executives. People with ADHD who need reliable capture without cognitive overhead. Capsule is for anyone whose work depends on confidential conversations.

06 — GET STARTED

Quiet tools do the best work.

Download Capsule. Free local transcription, no account required. Cloud credits when you need them, at a price you can verify.
