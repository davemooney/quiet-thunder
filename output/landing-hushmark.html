01

Your audio never leaves.

Other tools upload your voice to process it. Hushmark doesn't. Transcription runs on your machine using on-device AI. There is no server between your voice and your transcript. This isn't a privacy setting. It's the architecture.

02

An AI strategist in your ear.

While you're still in the meeting, Hushmark analyses the live transcript and whispers back: questions to ask, contradictions to probe, action items forming in real time. You glance down. You ask the perfect question. You look like you've been preparing all week.

05

No bot joins your call.

Hushmark captures audio through your system's audio layer — not by joining as a participant. No one on the call knows you're recording. No awkward "Hushmark Bot has joined the meeting" notification. No permissions to request. You open Hushmark, click record, and it listens to exactly what you hear. Zoom, Google Meet, Teams, FaceTime, phone calls, podcasts, lectures. If sound leaves your speaker, Hushmark can transcribe it.

06

People with the most to protect.

Hushmark was designed for professionals whose conversations carry weight — where a misquoted sentence can move markets, break trust, or compromise a case.

Journalists

Source protection is non-negotiable. Hushmark keeps recordings on your device, encrypted, never cloud-synced. When the story matters, the transcript stays yours.

Lawyers

Attorney-client privilege requires tools that respect confidentiality by design. No third-party servers. No data-sharing clauses. Full local control.

Researchers

Interview transcripts are research data. IRB compliance is simpler when your transcription tool never transmits participant audio to an external server.

Executives

Board conversations, M&A discussions, and strategy calls belong to you. Hushmark ensures sensitive discussions never appear in a vendor's training data.
